About Me

Turning challenges
into growth stories

I'm passionate about turning challenges into growth stories. With an MBA from IIM Kozhikode and an Integrated M.Sc. from NISER — featuring two peer-reviewed research papers and a contribution to a book published by Springer — I bring a blend of strategy, tech, and the mindset of a competitive problem solver to every project.

Currently at EY Parthenon, I specialize in procurement transformation, direct cost reduction, and market entry strategy. Before consulting, I co-founded GAMERS TAG — a Gaming Super App for Esports & Gaming — where I led strategy, finance, and operations from zero to 4X social media growth.

My journey spans from researching spectroscopy at NISER and BARC, to a fintech internship at RBI's Department of Supervision, to government consulting in Kerala — giving me a rare 360° perspective on how organizations create value.

Peer-Reviewed Journal Paper
